Coffee Cultivation in North Kivu

North Kivu, a region in the Democratic Republic of Congo, is known for its rich volcanic soil, which provides ideal conditions for coffee cultivation. Local farmers primarily grow Arabica coffee, celebrated for its smooth flavor and fruity notes. Despite challenges such as political instability and limited infrastructure, coffee production remains a key economic activity, supporting many small-scale farmers.
