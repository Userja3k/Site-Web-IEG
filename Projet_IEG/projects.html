Kananga faces significant waste management challenges, affecting both the environment and public health. To address this, we are launching a waste collection initiative aimed at cleaning the city and raising awareness about proper waste disposal.

Promoting Responsible Mineral Extraction

Our initiative focuses on the legal and ethical extraction of coltan in the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Partnering with regulatory bodies, we ensure sustainable mining practices that respect environmental standards and support local communities.
